Jeffrey Siler

Jeffrey B. Siler is the Senior Partner and lead trial attorney at Siler & Ingber, LLP. With more than 25 years of experience and over 100 jury trials to his credit, Jeffrey has dedicated his career to representing individuals injured in car accidents, truck accidents, slip-and-fall incidents, and other serious personal injury matters throughout New York.

Known as a true trial lawyer, Jeffrey has successfully litigated cases in courts across New York City and Long Island, earning a reputation for aggressively advocating for accident victims and pursuing the maximum compensation available on their behalf.

A graduate of New York Law School, where he served on the Law Journal, Jeffrey is also a frequent lecturer for the Firemen's Association of the State of New York and has been featured in media outlets including The New York Times, Newsday, ABC, and CBS.
